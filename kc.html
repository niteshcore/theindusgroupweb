Cargo Terminology Guides

Quick references for standard maritime and air freight abbreviations.

Airlines Terms

  • AWB (Air Waybill)
    A non-negotiable contract document issued by an air carrier detailing cargo descriptions and routing directions.
  • Cargo Manifest
    A comprehensive list of all cargo items loaded on a particular flight vessel.
  • IATA Standards
    Regulations governing safe transport of cargo, baggage, and hazardous substances set by the International Air Transport Association.
More on IATA Hub

Shipping & Sea Freight

  • B/L (Bill of Lading)
    A legal document issued by a maritime carrier acknowledging receipt of cargo and serving as title of ownership.
  • FOB (Free On Board)
    Standard Incoterm specifying that the seller is responsible for cargo delivery up to the loading dock of the vessel.
  • TEU (Twenty-foot Equivalent Unit)
    A standard unit of measurement used to determine the capacity of cargo container vessels based on 20-foot long containers.
